# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-01-12

## Summary

ManageDle respects your privacy. This document explains what information we collect, how we use it, your rights, and the terms for using this service.

---

## What We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address**: Used for authentication and account recovery
- **Username**: Your display name on the platform
- **Password**: Stored as a secure hash 
- **Profile information**: Avatar/profile picture (optional), display preferences, theme settings

### Usage & Game Data
- **Game records**: Scores, dates played, puzzle attempts, streaks
- **Social data**: Followers, following relationships, group memberships, social interactions
- **Usage analytics**: Page views, feature usage, errors (when analytics enabled)

### Technical Data
- **Cookies**: Essential cookies for session management and authentication
- **Local storage**: Game preferences, theme settings, UI state
- **Analytics cookies**: Anonymous usage data (Vercel Analytics) when enabled

## How We Use Your Data

- **Provide the service**: Authentication, score tracking, leaderboards, social features
- **Improve the product**: Diagnose issues, understand feature usage, fix bugs
- **Communication**: Account-related notifications (if implemented)
- **Security**: Detect and prevent abuse, spam, or unauthorized access

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

## Data Storage & Third Parties

- **Supabase**: Handles authentication, database storage, and user data. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **Vercel**: Hosts the application and provides analytics (when enabled). See [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Your browser**: Local data (preferences, theme) stored in localStorage; you can clear this anytime.

---

## Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies:

- **Essential cookies**: Required for authentication, sessions, and basic site functionality
- **Analytics cookies**: Vercel Analytics (or similar) collects anonymous usage data to help us improve the product. You can opt out by disabling third-party cookies in your browser or using ad-blocking extensions.
- **Common ID / Advertising**: If advertising integrations are enabled in the future, a Common ID cookie may be used for ad delivery and cross-device matching. Users can opt out via browser settings or partner opt-out links.

**How to opt out**: Disable cookies in your browser settings, use privacy-focused browser extensions, or contact the site owner to request analytics be disabled for your deployment.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: View your profile, game data, and social connections in the app
- **Update**: Modify your username, avatar, and preferences at any time
- **Delete**: Request account deletion by contacting the site owner (see below). This will remove your account, profile, and associated data. Note: Some data may be retained in backups for a limited time.
- **Export**: Request a copy of your data by contacting the site owner
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics tracking via browser settings or request the site owner disable it

For EU users: You have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
